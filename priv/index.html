TX Cache Contents

To explore some Erlang term, in your Erlang shell type tx:show(X). where X is the value to display. The term will be saved in tx's ETS table and you will get a (hopefully) clickable URL in your console window, or you can copy it and open in browser. Or refresh this page and click the corresponding show button.
